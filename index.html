Convert your Passion to Code

We are a youth-lead and youth-run community organization aiming our sights at addressing the underserved area of computer science education in our communities. We offer free coding workshops around the Lancaster county area for middle school and high school aged students. Love to code? Never written a line in your life? We welcome all skill levels. Code by the Students, for the Students

We host free coding workshops in Lancaster County to give middle school and high school aged students access to real-life developers and instruction, something many school districts in Lancaster County lack. This is the area which we aim to fill!

Our Philosophy of Free

We always want Convert to Code to be accessible, and that means accessible to everyone. We never want anything, be it skill level or money, to stand in the way of someone's opportunity to learn and grow. We believe that learning to code is a vital part of our increasingly tech saturated lives. Whether you are selling shoes or building airplanes, you need engineers and coders, so having a basic understanding of how computers operate, not only demystifies them, but increases your awareness of what is possible.
