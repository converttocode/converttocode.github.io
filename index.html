Convert to Code is a community organization that strives to facilitate learning of computer science and computer programming in the Lancaster, Pennsylvania area. Our vision is to provide a resource for local individuals to learn about coding and computer science in the increasingly tech saturated society we live in, targeting middle school and high school aged students.
