about

Convert to Code is a youth-run community organization that strives to facilitate learning of computer science and computer programming in the Lancaster County area. Our vision is to provide a resource for local individuals to learn about coding and computer science in the increasingly tech saturated society we live in, targeting high school aged students.

events

Convert to Code facilitates learning by hosting coding workshops around the Lancaster County area at various venues. We recruit instructors from the surrounding community to help us, including freelancers, professors, and computer science students. All events are free of charge.
